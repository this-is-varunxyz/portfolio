Hill Climb Clone

I was getting bored in my physics class, so I decided to recreate Hill Climb Racing for my Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) project. After watching several videos about how Hill Climb Racing was developed and learning about its history, I was inspired by the original developer to build my own version. Integrating engineering principles into the gameplay to create an educational and engaging experience.
